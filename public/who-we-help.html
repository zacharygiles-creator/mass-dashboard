The Inspection Workflow
No interpretation required. Just observation — the platform handles the rest.

MASSCORE's inspection workflow is built around structured rubric questions — condition labels that any qualified technician can apply consistently. You don't assign a score. You describe what you see. The platform converts your observation into a scored condition entry, weighted by asset criticality, and rolled up into the Plant Condition Score automatically.

Step 01
Select the site
Log in and pull up the facility. Your assigned systems and assets are already mapped and waiting.
Step 02
Walk the asset
Answer rubric questions. Log compressor readings, pressures, temps, and amp draw. Flag open findings with severity.
Step 03
Score updates live
Your inputs hit the engine. The Plant Condition Score updates. The client dashboard reflects the new state of the facility in real time.
