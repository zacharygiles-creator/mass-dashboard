The Methodology
Two scores. One number. Built on 30 years of field experience in the most complex mechanical environment in existence.

MASSCORE is a proprietary mechanical assessment scoring system developed by industrial ammonia refrigeration technicians. If the methodology holds up in an ammonia machine room — it holds up anywhere. Every asset class. Every failure mode. Every reading that matters.

MASS
Plant Health Score
M
Maintenance
How well is this plant being taken care of right now. The health score responds directly to the work being done — or not being done. Defer it, the score falls. Address it, the score recovers.
A
Active Condition
Every operational parameter that reflects the current state of the equipment. Compressor readings, oil temperatures, filter differentials, amp draw — the live pulse of the plant.
S
Severity
Open findings scored and weighted by how much they are damaging the plant right now. The roadmap tells you exactly which repairs move the score the most and what it costs to wait.
S
System Response
The health score is dynamic. It moves with your team's work. It rises when repairs are made and falls when maintenance is deferred. It is the score that justifies the next service contract renewal.
The Plant Health Score answers one question: how well is this facility being maintained right now? It responds to your team's work — and it is the score that justifies the next service contract renewal.
CORE
Plant Integrity Score
C
Core
The structural foundation of the asset. Physical condition, build quality, service life status, and repair history. What this equipment IS — independent of how well it is being maintained.
O
Origin
Where the asset started and what it has been through. Age, documented repairs, material degradation, and irreversible wear. The integrity score cannot be recovered by replacing components — it reflects the history of the machine itself.
R
Residual
What is left. The remaining structural value of the asset after accounting for age, condition, and deterioration. The ceiling of what this equipment can ever be regardless of the maintenance applied to it.
E
Engine
The intelligence layer. The algorithm built on 30 years of combined field experience in industrial ammonia refrigeration — the most demanding mechanical environment in existence. If the methodology holds up here, it holds up anywhere.
The Plant Integrity Score answers one question: what is the ceiling of this asset's potential regardless of how well it is maintained? It degrades with age, repair history, and structural deterioration. It cannot be recovered by replacing components.
MASSCORE
Plant Health Score (MASS) + Plant Integrity Score (CORE) = Plant Condition Score
The Product
A live platform. Real data. Deployed today.

MASSCORE is not a concept. It is a working software platform currently deployed at select industrial refrigeration and HVAC facilities across the United States — displaying Plant Condition Scores, system health breakdowns, active findings, compliance status, and a Score Improvement Roadmap that tells every facility owner exactly what to fix and what it costs.

Full platform launch is scheduled for Fall 2026. Early access is available now for qualified refrigeration and HVAC contractors and facility operators.
